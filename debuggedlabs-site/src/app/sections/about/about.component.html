WHO WE ARE

Debugged Labs is a a podcast-blog founded by two UCLA grads who resent gobbledygook. Our mission is to break down what's happening in technology, science, and society into byte-sized chunks that, well, make sense to the public. The world is fast-changing and the people building tech and gadgets aren't always thinking about what it means for the rest of us.

We're here to ask those tough questions, and have a bit of fun while we're at it.
